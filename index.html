About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Mariana Macedo

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Center for Collective Learning and Natural Intelligence Institute (ANITI) led by Dr. César Hidalgo.

In my current position, I have been developing new digital platforms to collect data of individual preferences regarding public policies proposed by presidential candidates in multiple countries (MonProgramme2022.org and Brazucracia.org).

I am interested in Human Dynamics, Gender, Race & Socioeconomic Inequalities, Data-Driven applications, Complex Systems, Urban Systems, Optimization, Swarm Intelligence, Evolutionary Computation and Multi-many objective frameworks.

I was born in Brasília, Brazil, in 1992. I received my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Exeter in 2022, and the B.Sc. and M.Sc degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Pernambuco in 2016 and 2017, respectively. I studied one extra year at Pace University as a non-degree international student sponsored by Brazil Scientific Mobility Program Fully-Funded Scholarship Recipient in 2015.
